    About

    I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Texas at Austin, working with Prof. David Z. Pan and co-advised by Prof. Ray T. Chen. Previously, I received the B.E. degree in microelectronic science and engineering from Fudan University, in 2018.

    My current research interests include

    • Machine Learning and Optimization
    • Efficient Algorithm and Architecture Design for High-Performance AI
    • Software/Hardware Co-design
    • Next-Generation AI Computing with Emerging Technology
    • Parallel Computing and GPU Acceleration for VLSI Design Automation
